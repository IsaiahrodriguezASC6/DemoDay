About Us

FirstLevel is an online market specializing in video games. We made this site because many companies are going downhill

because of the fact that technology is making your purchases more convient to come by. For example why go to a Toys R us when

Amazon can bring you anything you want whenever as well as wherever. Blockbuster is another example of technology taking

over; you wouldn't want to get in your car or a bus to go to a store just to get a movie if it hasn't already been taken. Netflix and

YouTube are the king and queen of digital media and has made lives much easier. This is what we want to do with video games.

We don't want to put any companies out of business but we want to make lives much easier to not only get a game (rare or not)

that they want, but show them games that they might really like. Many other online markets show you things that you already saw

multiple times or products that they really want to sell, not becaue you want it, but because they want to get rid of it. We want to

show you things that we feel you will really want. If statistics are showing that you are not really interested in something for

example, Mario games, expect to not see it until you start showing interest, and even when you do we won't shove it in your face.
