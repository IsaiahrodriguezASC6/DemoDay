Got a Question? We May Have The Answer Document

•What is this website about?

This website is an online market for games and game accesories. We as a team do not own these items; we bring you to sellers who may have the item you want.

•How is this different from Amazon or eBay?

FirstLevel is very similar to other marketing companies but we focus more on the recommendation side instead of showing you deals you'll never need or just showing you your recently viewed multiple times.

•How do I sell an item and ship it?

Check out our selling page for intructions on how to sell an item.

•What items can I sell?

Games from any console, and game accesories such as (headphones, controllers, chargers, grips, monitors, and game codes).

•What condition should the items be in?

Items should be in good or better condition if you want to sell it, if the item is broken you must mention it in the discription.

•How long does shipping usually take?

Shipping is estimated by where each product is coming from; if it's coming from one of the states it shouldn't take too long but if it's from out of the country it could take up to more time than expected.
